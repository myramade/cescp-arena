# Operation Hardened Node

A team challenge for the CESCP Embedded Systems Cybersecurity Bootcamp.

You and your team defend one embedded device across all four days of the bootcamp. Make the right security call, seal the node, and earn points. The team with the most points across all four days wins.

**Play here:** [Open the game][(https://myramade.github.io/cescp-arena/)]

## What you need

- A laptop with internet access
- Your team, sitting together
- The game link from your instructor

## Get started

1. Open the game link on the one laptop your team will use.
2. Click **Play as a team**.
3. Type your team name. Use the **same name and the same laptop** every time, so your points from each day add up.
4. You will see four day challenges. Play the day your instructor picks.

## How a mission works

- Read the short scenario. It puts your device in a real situation.
- Pick the control you think is right from the choices.
- **Right answer:** a link on the node seals, you earn points, and a short note explains why. Read it, that is the lesson.
- **Wrong answer:** it shows what an attacker would do, then lets you try again. Talk it over and pick another.

## How you score

- You earn points for each correct answer.
- You earn more for getting it right on the **first try** and for answering **quickly**. Think it through, but do not stall.
- Answer several in a row on the first try to build a streak bonus.
- Your points from every day add together into **one team total**. That total is your score on the board. If you replay a day, your best score counts.

## Win the day, then the week

- Finish all four days to complete your campaign.
- Watch the shared scoreboard on the main screen. Your team appears and moves up on its own as you finish each day.

## Team tips

- Talk before you answer. The wrong choices are real mistakes, so debate them.
- Read every reveal. It teaches the same point as the lecture, in one short paragraph.
- Shortcuts: press **A**, **B**, **C**, or **D** to answer, and **N** for the next mission.
- One person drives the laptop, but the whole team decides.

---

> **Goal:** Harden your node on every link, across all four days, faster and smarter than the other teams.
