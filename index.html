FOUR DAYS, ONE NODE, EVERY TEAM

Operation Hardened Node A four-day review arena. Each team plays. The best-hardened node wins.

Every team jumps on their own device and picks a name. Then they complete each day's challenge, Day 1 through Day 4. The points from every day are tracked together into one team total, and the host board ranks every team by that total.

For each team

Play as a team

Open on your laptop, pick your team name, and work through the four day challenges at your own pace. The node teaches you as you go, and your day scores keep adding up into one running total.

For the instructor

Host scoreboard

Project this on the room screen. As each team finishes and calls out a score, type it in. The board ranks every team live, with a podium for the top three.

Heads up: this version runs fully offline on each device, with no shared server, so the host board is where the scores come together. That keeps it reliable in any classroom with no setup.
